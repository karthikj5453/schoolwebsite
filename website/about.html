About AHS Wadgaon

Empowering Tribal Students Through Quality Education Since Our Founding

Our Foundation

AHS Wadgaon was established with the primary objective of providing accessible and quality education to tribal children in the Indravelli region of Adilabad District, Telangana. As an Ashram School, our foundation is rooted in the vision of holistic development, integrating academic learning with cultural preservation and community welfare.

Operating under the Tribal Development Department, we serve as a beacon of hope for tribal communities, ensuring that every child has access to education regardless of their socio-economic background. Our residential facility provides a safe and nurturing environment where students can focus on their studies while staying connected to their cultural roots.

Our Journey

2008

School Establishment

AHS Wadgaon was established with 150 students and 8 dedicated teachers, beginning our mission to serve tribal communities.

2012

Infrastructure Development

Introduction of new hostel facilities and the implementation of the Midday Meal Program to support student welfare.

2016

Academic Excellence

Achieved 95% pass rate in board examinations and received recognition for outstanding cultural activities and tribal art promotion.

2020

Digital Transformation

Expansion of facilities to include modern science laboratories and computer centers, embracing digital education.

2024

Continuing Legacy

Today, we continue our legacy of empowering tribal youth through education, serving over 500 students with 25+ faculty members.
